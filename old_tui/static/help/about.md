`Sova` is a free and open source project (GPL-3 license). It is developed by a small team of contributors, with love and care. `Sova` has good friends in the `Cookie Collective` (France) and in the `TOPLAP` community (international). Learn more about `Sova` on `https://sova.livecoding.fr`. `Sova` is an ongoing project aimed at creating a bespoke platform for live coding and algorithmic composition. It is used to play experimental music, to teach live coding, to explore algorithmic expression of ideas, to create music with friends.
